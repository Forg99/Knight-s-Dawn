# Knight-s-Dawn
Final School Project

This project is my rendition of Asteroids. Asteroids is a 1979 multidirectional shooter video game developed and published by Atari, Inc. for arcades.

Concept: the player controls a spaceship that has to shoot asteroids flying at and around the player in a confined looping part of space without getting hit by the asteroids or bullets. Asteroids split into smaller asteroids upon being destroyed.

Goal:The player has 3 lives, Destroy as many asteroids as possible before dying. Score is gained when destroying asteroids based on the size of the destroyed asteroid.

If the player has lives remaining after dying they will respawn in the middle of the play area.

Controls / movement: Spacebar shoots projectiles (bullets) in the direction the player is facing. arrow up key accelerates the player forward. Careful, there's no stopping in space left and right arrow keys rotate the player.

GameOver: Upon dying 3 times score can no longer be gained and a restart button will appear. Hitting the restart button will reset score and lives as well as the play area.
